Frequently Asked Questions

What are your facility hours?

Our gym is open from 6AM-9AM & 5PM-7PM. We are open for all weekdays and holidays except wednesday.

What types of equipment do you have available? Do you offer group classes?

We have a wide range of state-of-the-art equipment, including cardio machines, weightlifting equipment, and functional training areas. Additionally, we offer a variety of group classes such as yoga, spin, and HIIT, led by certified instructors.

What types of memberships do you offer? What are the rates?

We offer various membership options to suit different needs, including monthly, quarterly, and annual memberships. Rates vary depending on the duration and type of membership. Please contact us directly at nearby information cenmtres provided by the gym for current pricing information.

What are the benefits of being a member?

Membership includes access to all of our facilities and equipment, as well as unlimited group classes. Members also receive discounts on personal training sessions, access to exclusive member events, and personalized fitness consultations.

Can memberships be frozen or put on hold?

Yes, members can freeze their membership for up to 2 months per year for a small fee. This option is ideal for individuals who are temporarily unable to utilize the gym due to travel or other commitments.

What is your cancellation policy?

Members can cancel their membership at any time 30 day-notice prior to their next billing cycle. Please note that early termination fees may apply.

Is parking available for members?

Yes, we offer free parking for members in our designated parking area.
